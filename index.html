Elgin Stray Cat Map

A spatial mapping application built for the Elgin community — turning scattered reports of missing and stray cats into one shared, living map.

Sightings used to live in social media threads and word of mouth, so rescue efforts overlapped and leads went cold. The map gives every report a location, a time, and a status, letting volunteers focus search areas, spot patterns, and coordinate rescues around current information instead of fragments.

  • Community-maintained sighting reports, mapped in real time
  • Search coordination around shared spatial data
  • Shorter path from sighting to safe recovery

Automated Quality Control Software

A custom QC application that systematically identifies missing data points and tracks time metrics — replacing manual oversight with measurable process.

Reviewing municipal datasets by hand was slow, repetitive, and left no record of what was checked or how long it took. This tool scans datasets automatically, flags gaps for correction, and measures the review process itself — so data integrity is verified, not assumed.

  • Systematic detection of missing data points
  • Built-in time metrics across the review workflow
  • Manual oversight eliminated from the QC process
